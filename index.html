Thanks for Downloading our Theme, Follow the below steps to setup your blog.

  • Installation

    How to Create a New Blog?

    1 Go to blogger.com

    2 Click Create Your Blog and Sign in with your Google Account.

    3 See the below video for better consideration.

    How to Upload a Blogger Theme?

    1 Click on Theme.

    2 Click on Backup / Restore.

    3 Click on Choose File Select the File in XML Format which is given in Zip File (Extract the Zip file into folder first).

    4 Click on Upload, That's it.

  • Customization

    1 Click on Theme.

    2 Click on Customise.

    3 For Edit & change the theme colors, images, fonts & much more, see the below video and enjoy this awesome creativity.

  • Switch Menu

    For adding your links and icons in the Switch menu. So follow the below steps.

    1 Go to Theme >> Edit HTML.

    2 Click inside the HTML Box at once for focus and press CTRL+F (For Calling Hidden Search Box) then search 'header-logo' and replace the Text in Blue color with your Menu Icon name and Text in Red color with your Menu text name.

  • Profile & Cover Photo

    1 Go to Layout.

    2 Click on Edit.

    3 Click on Remove Image

    4 Click on Choose File to Upload Image. OR From the Web. Past an Image URL below.

    5 Click on Save.

    6 NOTE : Apply this same method to change Profile Image.

  • About

    1 Go to Theme >> Edit HTML.

    2 Click inside the HTML Box and press CTRL+F then search 'about-box' & and replace the text information in paragraph < p > tag with your info.

    3 If you want to add more information about you then search 'about-box-overlay' & and replace the text information in paragraph < p > tag with your info.

  • Skills

    1 Go to Theme >> Edit HTML.

    2 Click inside the HTML Box and press CTRL+F then search 'skills' & and replace the Red text with your skills.

  • Social Icons

    1 Go to Theme >> Edit HTML.

    2 Click inside the HTML Box and press CTRL+F then search 'social' & and replace the # with your social pages link then replace the Text in Blue color with your Social Page Icon name and Text in Red color with your Social Page name.

  • Social Connections

    1 Go to Theme >> Edit HTML.

    2 Click inside the HTML Box and press CTRL+F then search 'social-connection' & and replace the Text in Red color with your Social Page name.

    3 To change the Cover & Profile Image, replace the image URL with your Image URL .

    4 NOTE : Apply this same method to add more Pages.

  • Meta Tags for SEO

    1 Click on Theme >> Edit HTML >> Click anywhere in the HTML Editor and press CTRL+F for Search Box which will be appear on right top side of HTML Editor.

    2 Now, first fill the meta tags by reading it one by one with the information of your blog i.e. Meta Description and Keywords, Facebook and Twitter Graph.
